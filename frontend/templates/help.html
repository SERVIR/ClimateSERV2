Map page

  1. Select the layers icon at the top right of the panel
  2. Scroll until you find the desired data layer
  3. Check the box to add the layer to the display
  4. Uncheck the box to remove the layer
  5. Add as many layers as you find relevant to your study.

  1. Add layer to the map
  2. Zoom in to the area you are interested in
  3. Click the play button on the timeline control
  4. You may also grab and slide the handle to the date/time you would like
  5. You may use the previous, pause, and next buttons as needed.
  6. You may add multiple datasets to animate, if the time intervals are different they will animate as such. The more layers you animate, the more your network bandwidth may effect.
